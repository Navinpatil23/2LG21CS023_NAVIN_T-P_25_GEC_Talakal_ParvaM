History

Historical Overview: Bidar's history dates back to ancient times, with significant developments during the reign of the Bahmani Sultanate and later under the Mughal Empire. The city is renowned for its splendid architecture, including the impressive Bidar Fort, which stands as a testament to its rich past.

Timeline:

5th Century BCE: Establishment of Bidar
1427: Bidar becomes the capital of the Bahmani Sultanate
1538: Bidar annexed by the Kingdom of Bijapur
1686: Bidar becomes part of the Mughal Empire
1724: Incorporation into the Hyderabad State
