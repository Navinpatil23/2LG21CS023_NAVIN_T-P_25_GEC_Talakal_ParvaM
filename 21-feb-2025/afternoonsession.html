III. Place Desriptions:

Bdr Fort: This historic fort was built by Sultan Ahmad Shah I of the Bahmanid dynasty in 1427. It houses over 30 monuments, including palaces, mosques, and tombs

Solah Khamba Mosque: A 16-pillar mosque inside Bidar Fort.

Mahmud Gawan Madarsa: An ancient Islamic seminary.

Bahmani Tombs: Historical tombs of Bahmani rulers in Ashtur.

Chaubara: A 71-feet high cylindrical watchtower in the town center.

Nanak Jhira Sahib: A Sikh shrine with a natural spring.

Papnash Shiva Temple: A revered temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Gurudwara Shri Nanak Jhira Sahib: Another significant Sikh shrine.

Barid Shahi Tombs: Tombs of the Barid Shahi dynasty.
